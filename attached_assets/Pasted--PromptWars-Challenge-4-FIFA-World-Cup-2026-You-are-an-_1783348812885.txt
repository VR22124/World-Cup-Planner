# PromptWars Challenge 4 — FIFA World Cup 2026

You are an award-winning Product Designer, Software Architect, and Full Stack Engineer.

Your objective is to design and build the FIRST VERSION (MVP) of a project that has the potential to achieve a top score in PromptWars.

Do NOT over-engineer the application.

Build a polished, realistic MVP with clean architecture that can later be productionized.

# Challenge

Build a GenAI-enabled solution that enhances stadium operations and the overall tournament experience for fans, organizers, volunteers, or venue staff.

The solution must leverage Generative AI to improve one or more of the following:

• Stadium Navigation
• Crowd Management
• Accessibility
• Transportation
• Sustainability
• Multilingual Assistance
• Operational Intelligence
• Real-time Decision Support

during the FIFA World Cup 2026.

---

# Product Idea

The product is called

## StadiumIQ AI

StadiumIQ AI is an intelligent stadium operations and fan assistance platform powered by Generative AI.

Instead of being "just a chatbot", the AI continuously understands the current stadium situation and provides contextual recommendations for different users.

The platform serves multiple personas.

### Fan

The AI helps fans with

• finding the correct entrance
• navigation inside the stadium
• shortest food queues
• nearest restroom
• transportation guidance
• multilingual assistance
• accessibility assistance

### Operations Team

The AI monitors

• crowd density
• gate congestion
• incidents
• volunteer availability
• transport delays

and generates operational recommendations.

Example:

"Gate 5 is approaching unsafe capacity. Redirect arriving spectators to Gate 7."

### Volunteers

Volunteers receive

• assigned tasks
• navigation
• incident alerts
• AI-generated assistance recommendations
